1916 Tseng-Ying Pang grew up in Beijing since childhood to accept Chinese calligraphy education mother, and began to learn traditional painting, 18 years old to enter Peking Metropolitan College of Art and specializing in Western painting, and subsequently won a scholarship to study in Japan, after they have successfully entered the Japanese universities, after graduating from the Art Institute of returning to Beijing to teach.
